What is MBSR?

8-Week Program

Structured mindfulness training developed at UMass Medical School by Jon Kabat-Zinn.

Core Practices

Body scan meditation, sitting meditation, mindful movement, and daily mindfulness exercises.

Proven Benefits

Reduces stress, anxiety, depression symptoms, and improves emotional regulation and resilience.
